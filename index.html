SUJASH ISLAM PURNA is a Bangladeshi immigrant artist and PhD student in the Composition and Rhetoric program at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. He is the author of several books of poetry and creative nonfiction. Sujashâ€™s work is influenced by his immigrant, POC experience in America, especially as a first-generation immigrant/international student navigating the institutionalization and assimilation practices in the higher education discourse and curriculum. His research interests include decolonizing rhetoric, affective labor of the international body, transnational and translingual studies.

Currently, Sujash is teaching ENGL 100 - Introduction to College Composition at the University of Wisconsin-Madison as a Graduate Teaching Assistant. He previously taught at Missouri State University and at the K-12 level in the state of Missouri
